Welcome to My Analog Design Portfolio

Hi, I'm Randy. I hold a degree in Computer Engineering from the University of Waterloo with a strong focus on analog circuit design. My primary interests lie in precision analog systems, including operational amplifiers, bandgap voltage references, and circuits for audio and RF applications. My work bridges theory and application — from modelling and simulation to layout and prototyping. I strive to design robust, efficient, and scalable analog solutions that stand up to real-world performance requirements. Please explore my portfolio to learn more about the projects I’ve undertaken and the design challenges I’ve addressed.
